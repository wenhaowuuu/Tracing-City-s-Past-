Downtown San Jose is the central business district of San Jose, California, in Silicon Valley. It is the largest urban center of Silicon Valley. Downtown is one of the largest tech clusters in Silicon Valley, as well as the cultural and political center of San Jose.

North San Jose is the northern region of San Jose, California in Silicon Valley. North San Jose includes the communities of Alviso and Berryessa as well as the North San Jose Innovation District. It lends its name to the future Berryessa/North San Jose BART station. Neighborhoods within North San Jose.
